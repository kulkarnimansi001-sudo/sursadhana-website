Her Story

Biography

10+
Years of Teaching

An accomplished Hindustani Classical vocalist and dedicated music educator with over 10 years of teaching experience. She has trained numerous students across different age groups, helping them develop a strong foundation in Indian classical music while nurturing confidence and musical expression.

She has presented Indian Classical, Semi-Classical, and Light Vocal concerts at numerous prestigious festivals and cultural events across Maharashtra and various parts of India, and has been honoured with several awards and accolades throughout her musical journey.

Mansi is a devoted disciple of Pt. Shubhada Tai Paradkar, the renowned vocalist of the Agra-Gwalior Gharana, and continues to uphold the rich traditions of this distinguished musical lineage through both her performances and teaching.

The Path

Musical Journey

A lifelong commitment to music — from her earliest training to founding SurSadhana Music Classes.

Early Years
Beginning the Sadhana
Drawn to music from a young age, Mansi began her formal training in Hindustani Classical Vocal, developing a deep love for ragas, swaras, and the discipline of daily riyaz.
Higher Training
Discipleship Under Pt. Shubhada Tai Paradkar
Mansi became a devoted disciple of Pt. Shubhada Tai Paradkar, the renowned vocalist of the illustrious Agra-Gwalior Gharana. Under her Guru's guidance, she immersed herself in the classical traditions, layakari, and khayal bandishes of this distinguished lineage.
Recognition
Graded Artist — All India Radio
Her vocal prowess and dedication were formally recognized when she was awarded the prestigious Graded Artist status by All India Radio (Akashvani) — one of the highest recognitions for classical musicians in India.
Performances
Concerts Across India
Mansi has performed Indian Classical, Semi-Classical, and Light Vocal concerts at prestigious festivals and cultural events across Maharashtra and India, sharing the beauty of the Agra-Gwalior Gharana with audiences everywhere.
Teaching
Founded SurSadhana Music Classes
Driven by the desire to pass on the treasures of Indian classical music to the next generation, Mansi founded SurSadhana Music Classes in Pune — creating a nurturing space where students learn music as a lifelong sadhana, not just a skill.
Today
Inspiring the Next Generation
With 200+ students trained and numerous Gandharva exam toppers, Mansi continues to teach, perform, and inspire — organizing annual concerts, workshops, and cultural ceremonies that celebrate the richness of Indian classical music.

The Approach

Teaching Philosophy

Mansi's teaching is rooted in the belief that music is not merely technique — it is a path of self-discovery, discipline, and devotion.

🪘
Riyaz First
Daily practice is the foundation of musical growth. Every student is guided to build a strong habit of riyaz — the disciplined, mindful repetition that transforms technique into expression.
🎵
Personalized Guidance
Each student has a unique voice and pace. Mansi tailors her teaching to match each student's natural abilities, learning style, and goals — from young beginners to adult learners.
🙏
Preserving Tradition
The Agra-Gwalior Gharana's rich vocabulary of ragas, taans, and bandishes is passed on with care — ensuring that classical music continues to thrive with authenticity and depth.
🌱
Nurturing Confidence
Stage opportunities — annual concerts, competitions, and cultural events — are integral to the curriculum, helping students build confidence, presence, and love for performance.
🎓
Academic Excellence
Structured preparation for Gandharva Mahavidyalaya examinations ensures students build a solid academic foundation alongside their practical musical skills.
❤️
Samarpan
True music comes from the heart. Mansi inspires students to approach every raga with sincerity, reverence, and complete surrender to the art — Samarpan in its truest sense.
