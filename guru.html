Her Story

Biography

10+
Years of Teaching

Mansi Deshpande is a distinguished Hindustani Classical Vocalist, music educator, and founder of SurSadhana Music Classes, Pune. Born into a family of music lovers, her passion for music blossomed from an early age. She received her initial training under Smt. Anjali Deshpande and Pt. Vishwanath Dasharathe, and has been undergoing advanced taleem for over 13 years under Pt. Shubhada Paradkar, a renowned vocalist of the Gwalior–Agra–Jaipur gharanas.

She holds a Master's degree in Music from SNDT Women's University (First Rank · Gold Medal · 7 awards at convocation), is NET qualified, holds Sangeet Visharad and Sangeet Alankar from Akhil Bharatiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya, and is currently pursuing Sangeetacharya. She has also served with the Department of Lifelong Learning & Extension, SNDT University, Pune.

Mansi has been recognized with the NCPA Scholarship (2021–22) for emerging artists and is the recipient of the Pt. Gajananbua Joshi Award, Pt. Bhimsen Joshi Award, and the prestigious B High Grade by Akashvani (All India Radio) in Hindustani Classical Vocal — one of the highest recognitions for classical musicians in India.

The Path

Musical Journey

A lifelong commitment to music — from her earliest training to founding SurSadhana Music Classes.

Foundation
Initial Training & First Gurus
Born into a family of music lovers, Mansi's formal training began under Smt. Anjali Deshpande and Pt. Vishwanath Dasharathe — building the foundation of swara, laya, and disciplined riyaz that would define her musical path.
Advanced Taleem
Discipleship Under Pt. Shubhada Paradkar
For over 13 years, Mansi has been receiving advanced taleem from Pt. Shubhada Paradkar, a celebrated vocalist of the Gwalior–Agra–Jaipur gharanas. Under her Guru's guidance, she has immersed herself in the khayal traditions, intricate taans, and rare bandishes of this distinguished lineage.
Academic Excellence
M.A. Music — Gold Medal, First Rank · SNDT
Mansi secured First Rank in M.A. Music at SNDT Women's University, receiving seven awards including a Gold Medal at convocation. She is also NET qualified, holds Sangeet Visharad and Sangeet Alankar from Gandharva Mahavidyalaya, and served with SNDT's Department of Lifelong Learning & Extension.
2021–22
NCPA Scholarship for Emerging Artists
Selected for the prestigious National Centre for the Performing Arts (NCPA) Scholarship — awarded to exceptional emerging classical artists — recognizing her mastery, promise, and contribution to Hindustani classical music.
Awards
B High Grade — Akashvani · Pt. Bhimsen Joshi & Gajananbua Joshi Awards
Awarded the B High Grade by Akashvani (All India Radio) in Hindustani Classical Vocal — one of the most coveted recognitions for Indian classical musicians. Also the recipient of the Pt. Bhimsen Joshi Award and Pt. Gajananbua Joshi Award for excellence in classical music.
Performances
Concerts at Prestigious National Festivals
Mansi has performed at acclaimed platforms including Pt. Bhimsen Joshi Mahotsav, Ellora–Ajanta Festival, Krishnabai Sangeet Festival, and numerous national music festivals. She has appeared on DD Sahyadri (Swar Pravah, Music Mehfil) and performed across Pune, Mumbai, Nashik, Aurangabad, Lucknow, Indore, Bhubaneswar, Varanasi, and beyond.
Today
Founded SurSadhana · Inspiring the Next Generation
Driven by the Guru-Shishya parampara, Mansi founded SurSadhana Music Classes in Pune — a nurturing space where 200+ students of all ages learn music as a lifelong sadhana. She continues to perform, pursue Sangeetacharya, and preserve the rich heritage of Hindustani classical music.

The Approach

Teaching Philosophy

Mansi's teaching is rooted in the belief that music is not merely technique — it is a path of self-discovery, discipline, and devotion.

🪘
Riyaz First
Daily practice is the foundation of musical growth. Every student is guided to build a strong habit of riyaz — the disciplined, mindful repetition that transforms technique into expression.
🎵
Personalized Guidance
Each student has a unique voice and pace. Mansi tailors her teaching to match each student's natural abilities, learning style, and goals — from young beginners to adult learners.
🙏
Preserving Tradition
The Agra-Gwalior Gharana's rich vocabulary of ragas, taans, and bandishes is passed on with care — ensuring that classical music continues to thrive with authenticity and depth.
🌱
Nurturing Confidence
Stage opportunities — annual concerts, competitions, and cultural events — are integral to the curriculum, helping students build confidence, presence, and love for performance.
🎓
Academic Excellence
Structured preparation for Gandharva Mahavidyalaya examinations ensures students build a solid academic foundation alongside their practical musical skills.
❤️
Samarpan
True music comes from the heart. Mansi inspires students to approach every raga with sincerity, reverence, and complete surrender to the art — Samarpan in its truest sense.
