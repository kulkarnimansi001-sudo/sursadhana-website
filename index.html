Our Philosophy

About SurSadhana Music Classes

At SurSadhana Music Classes, we believe that music is not merely a skill — it is a lifelong journey of learning, discipline, and self-discovery. With structured training, personalized guidance, and a nurturing environment, we preserve the rich heritage of Indian classical music while helping every student discover their unique musical identity.

🪘

Sadhana

Consistent practice is the foundation of musical excellence. We encourage every student to cultivate dedication, patience, and disciplined Riyaz.

🎵

Sur

A deep understanding of melody, purity of notes, and musical expression forms the heart of our training — developing both technical proficiency and aesthetic sensitivity.

🙏

Samarpan

True learning blossoms through humility, commitment, and devotion to the art. We inspire students to approach music with sincerity, respect, and passion.

What We Teach

Our Courses

Structured training in Indian Classical Music with a strong emphasis on building a solid musical foundation through disciplined learning and regular practice.

🎤

Indian Classical Vocal

Gandharva Syllabus

Our primary course follows the examination syllabus of Akhil Bharatiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya Mandal, Vashi — guiding students from Prarambhik to the prestigious Sangeet Alankar level.

  • Voice culture & swara development
  • Raga presentation & layakari
  • Theory & performance skills
  • Examination-oriented preparation
🎹

Harmonium

Solo & Accompaniment

Learn Harmonium alongside vocal music, covering accompaniment techniques, solo performance, notation reading, and examination preparation as per the Gandharva syllabus.

  • Accompaniment techniques
  • Solo performance
  • Notation reading
  • Gandharva exam preparation
🎶

Marathi & Hindi Light Vocal

Bhavgeet · Natyasangeet

Dedicated batches in Marathi Bhavgeet, Bhaktigeet, Natyasangeet, Abhang, and Hindi Light Vocal — emphasizing expression, pronunciation, and stage presentation.

  • Marathi Bhavgeet & Bhaktigeet
  • Natyasangeet & Abhang
  • Hindi Light Vocal
  • Stage presentation skills
Whether your goal is academic pursuit, stage performance, or lifelong passion — our courses are designed to nurture students at every level.

Meet Your Guru

Mansi Kulkarni Deshpande

🎙️ B High Grade — Akashvani (All India Radio) · Hindustani Classical Vocal

A distinguished Hindustani Classical Vocalist and founder of SurSadhana Music Classes, Pune. Her first musical guru is her mother, Smt. Vaishali Kulkarni. She then received formal training under Smt. Anjali Deshpande and Pt. Vishwanath Dasharathe, and has been undergoing advanced taleem for over 13 years under Pt. Shubhada Paradkar, renowned vocalist of the Gwalior–Agra–Jaipur gharanas.

She holds an M.A. Music (Gold Medal, First Rank) from SNDT Women's University, is NET qualified, holds Sangeet Alankar from Gandharva Mahavidyalaya, and is the recipient of the NCPA Scholarship (2021–22), Pt. Bhimsen Joshi Award, and Pt. Gajananbua Joshi Award.

She has performed at prestigious platforms including Pt. Bhimsen Joshi Mahotsav, Ellora–Ajanta Festival, and Krishnabai Sangeet Festival, and has appeared on DD Sahyadri (Swar Pravah · Music Mehfil) across cities from Pune to Varanasi, Lucknow, Indore, and Bhubaneswar.
