Meet Your Guru

Mansi Kulkarni Deshpande

🎙️ B High Grade — Akashvani (All India Radio) · Hindustani Classical Vocal

A distinguished Hindustani Classical Vocalist and founder of SurSadhana Music Classes, Pune. Her first musical guru is her mother, Sau. Vaishali Kulkarni. She then received formal training under Smt. Anjali Deshpande and Pt. Vishwanath Dasharathe, and has been undergoing advanced taleem for over 13 years under Pt. Shubhada Paradkar, renowned vocalist of the Gwalior–Agra–Jaipur gharanas.

She holds an M.A. Music (Gold Medal, First Rank) from SNDT Women's University, is NET qualified, holds Sangeet Alankar from Gandharva Mahavidyalaya, and is the recipient of the NCPA Scholarship (2021–22), Pt. Bhimsen Joshi Award, and Pt. Gajananbua Joshi Award.

She has performed at prestigious platforms including Pt. Bhimsen Joshi Mahotsav, Ellora–Ajanta Festival, and Krishnabai Sangeet Festival, and has appeared on DD Sahyadri (Swar Pravah · Music Mehfil) across cities from Pune to Varanasi, Lucknow, Indore, and Bhubaneswar.
