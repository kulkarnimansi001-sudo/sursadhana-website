We offer training in Indian Classical Vocal, Harmonium, and Marathi & Hindi Light Vocal for children, students, and adults of all age groups.
We follow the syllabus of Akhil Bharatiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya Mandal, Vashi, preparing students from Prarambhik to Sangeet Alankar examinations.
Students 6 years and above are welcome to enroll. We welcome complete beginners as well as students with prior musical training.
Regular classes are conducted twice a week, along with a dedicated Riyaz (practice) session every weekend to strengthen learning and performance skills.
