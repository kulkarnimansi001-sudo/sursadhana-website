2. Fees & Payment

  • Monthly fees are due on or before the 7th of each month.

3. Refund & Cancellation Policy

Summary: Monthly fees are non-refundable once the month has started. Failed or duplicate online payments are refunded within 5–7 working days.

Monthly fees:

  • Monthly fees are non-refundable once the calendar month has begun, regardless of the number of classes attended.
  • If a student chooses to discontinue mid-month, no refund is issued for the remaining classes of that month.
  • If a student wishes to stop classes, they may do so at any time by informing us. No advance notice period is required. The discontinuation takes effect from the following month; no partial refund is provided for the current month.

School-initiated cancellations:

  • If SurSadhana Music Classes cancels a class due to a public holiday, teacher illness, or force majeure, a makeup class will be offered at the teacher's earliest convenience.
  • If a batch is permanently discontinued by the school, a pro-rata refund for the remaining days of the paid month will be issued within 10 working days.

Online payment failures & duplicates:

  • If a payment is deducted from your account but not recorded in the portal, please contact us within 48 hours with a screenshot. We will investigate and credit your account or issue a refund within 5–7 working days.
  • Duplicate payments (same fee paid twice) will be refunded in full within 5–7 working days after verification.
  • For disputes with Cashfree Payments, you may also contact them directly at cashfree.com/contact-us.
